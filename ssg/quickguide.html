Quick Guide

So you just got the game... What's next'?

If you experience any issues with the ESO Launcher, the installation or the game itself, check out the official ESO Help & Support page or the ESO Forum, lots of people already helped solve lots of problems there. You can also seek help in the ESO SubReddit or join our Discord Community. And there is always good old fashioned E-Mail aswell...

But let's start now with the actual game: The first step that you will come across is the character creation. Here you choose the name, gender, race and alliance (more on the latter two soon to come) for your character. After that, you get to choose your class (also, more soon to come) and then your looks. Oh, there's a lot to do...
